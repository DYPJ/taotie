## Australian

### The Deep Extragalactic VIsible Legacy Survey (DEVILS)

* The Deep Extragalactic VIsible Legacy Survey (DEVILS) is a spectroscopic campaign at the Anglo-Australian Telescope aimed at bridging the near and distant Universe by producing the highest completeness survey of galaxies and groups at intermediate redshifts (0.3 < z < 1.0).

- [Deep Extragalactic VIsible Legacy Survey (DEVILS): motivation,design, and target catalogue](https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2018MNRAS.480..768D/abstract)

### Taipan

- [The Taipan Galaxy Survey: Scientific Goals and Observing Strategy](https://arxiv.org/abs/1706.01246)

#### Conference

- [Cosmology with the Taipan survey](https://astronomy.swin.edu.au/~cblake/Taipan_Mar15.pdf)

### GALactic Archaeology with HERMES (GALAH) survey

* GALAH is a Large Observing Program using the HERMES instrument with the Anglo-Australian Telescope of the Australian Astronomical Observatory. HERMES provides simultaneous spectra for 400 objects in four wavelength bands tailored to obtain a range of chemical element lines.

- [The GALAH survey: scientific motivation](https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2015MNRAS.449.2604D/abstract)
- [The GALAH Survey: second data release](https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2018MNRAS.478.4513B/abstract)

### ATLAS Probe

- [ATLAS Probe - Astrophysics Telescope for Large Area Spectroscopy](https://atlas-probe.ipac.caltech.edu/page/mission-concept)
	- ATLAS Probe is a 1.5m telescope with a FoV of 0.4 sq deg, and uses DMDs as slit selectors with R=1000 spectroscopic resolution.
